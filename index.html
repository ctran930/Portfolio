How To Do an Oil Change

If you drive, you will need a oil change done to your vehicle every 3000-5000 miles.

Why not save money and do it yourself? All you need is a positive mindset and some tools. Heres how:

Parts and Equipment

  • Wrench
  • New Oil and Filter
  • Car Lift(Recommended) or Jack Stand
  • Drain Bucket
  • Cleaning supplies
    • Gloves
    • Towels

Steps

  1. Put your car in the air on a lift or jack stand
  2. Get the bucket ready to disposal of the used oil
  3. Use the wrench to unscrew the lock on oil container
  4. Let the oil drain and wait until it is at a drip drip motion
  5. While waiting, unscrew oil filter
  6. Add oil to the new filter and replace the old one
  7. Make sure the oil is fully drained and screw lock back on
  8. Drop the car down and add 5qt-6qts, depending on the car
  9. Check oil levels and clean up!
